Privacy Policy

Unvaped · Effective date: May 25, 2026

Unvaped (“the app,” “we,” “us,” “our”) is designed to help you track your progress quitting vaping. We built Unvaped to be private by default. This policy explains what information the app handles and how.

The short version

Unvaped does not collect, transmit, sell, or share your personal data. Everything you enter stays on your device.

Information stored on your device

To function, the app stores the following information locally on your device only:

  • Your quit date, streak history, and personal best
  • Cravings beaten, check-ins completed, and slips logged
  • Your reasons for quitting (“My Why”), including any photo you choose to add
  • Trigger events you record
  • App settings and reminder preferences

This information is stored on your device using Apple’s standard on-device storage. It is never sent to us or to any third party. We operate no servers and have no ability to access it. If you delete the app, this information is removed from your device.

Information we do NOT collect

  • We do not require an account, email address, or login.
  • We do not track you across other apps or websites.
  • We do not use third-party advertising or analytics services.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share any of your information.

Subscriptions and payments

Unvaped offers optional auto-renewable subscriptions (Unvaped Premium) purchased through the Apple App Store. All payments are processed by Apple. We never see or store your payment information. Apple’s handling of your purchase is governed by Apple’s own Privacy Policy.

Notifications

If you enable reminders, notifications are scheduled locally on your device. We do not send push notifications from any server.

Children’s privacy

Unvaped is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone.

Changes to this policy

If we update this policy, we will revise the effective date above and post the updated version at this URL.
