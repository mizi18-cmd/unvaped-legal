Unvaped

Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 26, 2026

Unvaped is designed to help you track your nicotine-free streak, cravings, reminders, motivations, journal entries, and progress. The app is built to keep your personal recovery data on your device.

Information Unvaped Stores

Unvaped may store the following information locally on your device:

  • Your quit date and streak progress.
  • Your weekly spend and vape-use estimates for savings calculations.
  • Your reason for quitting and optional motivation photo.
  • Your craving logs, trigger logs, check-ins, slips, journal entries, lessons progress, achievements, and app settings.
  • Your notification reminder preferences.

Local Storage

Unvaped stores app data locally on your device using iOS storage. Widgets may read limited streak information from the app's shared on-device container so they can display your streak. Unvaped does not operate a server for this data, does not sell it, and does not share it with advertisers.

Photos

If you choose to add a motivation photo, Unvaped uses the iOS photo picker so you can select an image. The selected image is stored locally for use inside the app. Unvaped does not upload your photo to an Unvaped server.

Notifications

If you enable reminders, Unvaped schedules local notifications on your device. You can turn reminders off in the app or in iOS Settings.

Subscriptions and Purchases

Unvaped+ subscriptions are processed by Apple through the App Store. Unvaped does not receive or store your payment card details. Apple may provide subscription status to the app so premium features can be unlocked.

Analytics, Advertising, and Tracking

Unvaped does not include third-party advertising, does not sell personal information, and does not track you across apps or websites. If this changes in the future, this policy and the App Store privacy details will be updated.

Health Disclaimer

Unvaped is a habit-tracking and motivation app. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. For medical questions or help quitting nicotine, consult a qualified healthcare professional.

Deleting Your Data

You can reset app data from the app's settings. You can also delete Unvaped from your device to remove locally stored app data associated with the installation.

Children's Privacy

Unvaped is not directed to children. The app is intended for users who are old enough to make decisions about nicotine cessation and related wellness tools in their location.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. If changes are made, the effective date above will be revised.
